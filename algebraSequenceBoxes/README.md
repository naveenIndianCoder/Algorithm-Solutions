### Algebra Sequence — Boxes - Create a function that takes: 

1. A **Positive Integer (Step)** as an argument. 
1. Shows the **Amount** of **Boxes** in that **Argument Step** of the **Sequence** in the console.
1. **Instructions** to **Follow**:
    * Step 0 : Start with 0.
    * Step 1 : Add 3.
    * Step 2 : Subtract 1.
    * Repeat Step 1 & 2...

> boxSequence(49) ➞ 51 

> boxSequence(12) ➞ 12

> boxSequence(19) ➞ 21
