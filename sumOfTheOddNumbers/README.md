### Sum of the Odd Numbers - Create a function that takes: 

1. A **Integer (n)** as an argument. 
1. Shows the **Total** of **All Odd Numbers Up To** and **Including** argument **n** in the console.

> sumOddNums(13) ➞ 49

> sumOddNums(20) ➞ 100

> sumOddNums(49) ➞ 625
