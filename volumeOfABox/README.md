### Volume of a Box - Create a function that takes: 

1. An **Object** with **Properties** as **Height**, **Width**, and **Length** of a **Box**, as an argument. 
1. Shows the **Volume** of the **Box** in the console.

> volumeOfTheBox({
  width: 2,
  length: 4,
  height: 20
}) ➞ 160 

> volumeOfTheBox({
  width: 7,
  length: 30,
  height: 5
}) ➞ 1050

> volumeOfTheBox({
  width: 10,
  length: 19,
  height: 6
}) ➞ 1140
