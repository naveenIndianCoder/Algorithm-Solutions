### WordCharWord - Create a function that takes:

#### Difficulty - Very Easy

1. A **Character** and A **String** as an argument. 
1. Puts the **Argument Character** between **Every Word** in the **Second Argument String** and shows the **Output** in the console.

> wordCharWord("@", "naveen sharma") ➞ "naveen@sharma" 

> wordCharWord("!", "javascript is my favorite  language") ➞ "javascript!is!my!favorite!!language"

> wordCharWord("+", "work hard or go home") ➞ "work+hard+or+go+home"
