### Area of A Rectangle - Create A Function that Takes:

#### Difficulty - Very Easy  

1. **Height** and **Width** as an argument.
1. Shows **Area of Rectangle** in the **Console**.
1. Otherwise shows **-1**, If the **Arguments** are **Invalid**.

> rectArea(44, 27) ➞ 1188  

> rectArea(42, 0) ➞ -1

> rectArea(20, 21) ➞ 420
