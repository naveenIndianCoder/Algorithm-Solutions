### Fix Basic Calculator - Create A Function that Takes:

#### Difficulty - Very Easy

1. **Two Numbers** and **A Operator (+, -, *, /) as String** as an argument.
1. Shows the **Result** of the **Argument Mathematical Expression** in the **Console**.

> fixingBasicCalculator(37, "*", 2021) ➞ 74777

> fixingBasicCalculator(2 "/", 9) ➞ 0.2222222222222222 

> fixingBasicCalculator(379, "-", 21) ➞ 358

Note: 
1. Shows **null** in the **Console**, If Any of the **Arguments** are **0** or **Operator** is **Not One** of the **Argument Characters**.
