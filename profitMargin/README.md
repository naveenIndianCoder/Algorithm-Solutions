### Profit Margin - Create a function that takes: 

1. **Sales** and **Cost Prices** as an argument. 
1. Shows the **Progit Margin** as a **Percentage Formated String** and **Rounded** to **One Decimal** in the console.

> calculateProfitMargin(79, 57) ➞ "27.8%" 

> calculateProfitMargin(538, 250) ➞ "53.5%"

> calculateProfitMargin(1019, 120) ➞ "88.2%"

Note: Profit Margin - (Sales Price - Cost Price) / Sales Price * 100
